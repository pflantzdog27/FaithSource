About FaithSource

FaithSource is a platform that connects diverse religious voices with broadcast and print bookers, producers and journalists. FaithSource participants include religious leaders and people of faith who are positioned to change the national conversation on values issues, countering voices of intolerance and injustice.

FaithSource responds to a need expressed by journalists, bookers and producers for a broader range of faith voices who can discuss social issues including economic justice, environmentalism, foreign policy, LGBT equality, health care, immigration, racial equality, and gender justice.

FaithSource offers journalists faith voices who can and will talk directly into a compelling faith frame about the pressing issues that face our country and our world.
